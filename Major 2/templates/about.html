About SkinDerm AI

Revolutionizing dermatological diagnosis with artificial intelligence

Our Mission

SkinDerm AI was created to make dermatological expertise accessible to everyone. Our AI-powered platform provides instant preliminary assessments of skin conditions, helping users make informed decisions about their skin health.

Technology

Using state-of-the-art Vision Transformer (ViT) models trained on thousands of dermatological images, our system identifies patterns with accuracy comparable to board-certified dermatologists.

Important Disclaimer

SkinDerm AI provides informational suggestions only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a healthcare provider for diagnosis and treatment.
